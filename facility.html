Our Facilities

Discover our state-of-the-art facilities, equipped with the latest tools and technology to ensure top-quality and manufacturing supplies

Area:

The automotive division of PMTechno is built across a dedicated space of approximately 50,000 sq. ft., specially designed to support the development and production of automotive components. The facility is segmented into areas for sheet metal processing, assembly, painting, and storage. The well-organized layout ensures a logical flow of operations — from raw material intake to finished product output — minimizing handling time and optimizing productivity.

Power Press:

This unit is equipped with mechanical and hydraulic power presses ranging from 10 to 250 tons. These machines are used for stamping, punching, bending, and pressing sheet metal parts that are critical to vehicle manufacturing. Common components produced include body parts, chassis supports, brackets, and structural frames. The automotive power press area is designed for both low-volume prototyping and high-volume production.

Quality Control:

The automotive quality control section uses advanced equipment like coordinate measuring machines (CMM), vernier calipers, micrometers, dial gauges, surface finish testers, and hardness testers to ensure all manufactured parts meet safety and industry standards. Each part undergoes multiple inspection checkpoints, including dimensional accuracy and mechanical strength tests. A Pre-Delivery Inspection (PDI) setup is used to simulate real-world performance before shipping to clients.

Area:

The industrial manufacturing division spans over 50,000 sq. ft., structured to handle bulk production of heavy-duty components and industrial-grade machinery parts. The facility layout includes zones for raw material storage, metal forming, welding, surface treatment, and finished goods inventory. It is optimized for large-scale fabrication projects and high-load equipment handling

Powder Coating:

We provide advanced powder coating solutions tailored for industrial applications. This finishing process involves applying a dry powder to metal surfaces using an electrostatic method, followed by heat curing to form a durable, uniform coating. Our powder coating enhances the longevity, corrosion resistance, and surface strength of industrial components such as machinery frames, control panels, enclosures, brackets, and structural parts. It offers superior protection against harsh environmental conditions, chemical exposure, and mechanical wear—making it ideal for heavy-duty industrial use.

Laser Cutting:

We offer precision laser cutting services designed to meet the demands of industrial manufacturing. Using advanced CNC-controlled laser technology, we can accurately cut complex shapes and patterns on a wide range of materials, including mild steel, stainless steel, and aluminum. Our laser cutting process ensures high accuracy, clean edges, and minimal material waste, making it ideal for producing components like machine panels, enclosures, brackets, and customized metal parts. Whether it's for prototyping or large-scale production, our solutions are tailored to deliver efficiency, consistency, and quality.

Area:

The tool room occupies about 10,000 sq. ft., organized into CNC machining centers, conventional machines, tool assembly zones, and tool storage racks. This controlled environment supports fine machining, tool designing, and maintenance tasks. The area layout ensures low vibration, high stability, and dust control to maintain tool precision and cleanliness.

Vertical Machining Centres

Vertical Machining Centres play a vital role in the precision manufacturing of automotive components. These advanced machines are designed for high-speed, accurate operations, allowing us to produce complex parts with tight tolerances and exceptional consistency. By utilizing Vertical Machining Centres, we ensure efficient production processes, reduced lead times, and high-quality output that meets the rigorous standards of the automotive industry. Their reliability and performance contribute significantly to maintaining the excellence of our products.

Wire Cutting EDM

Tool accuracy is vital, and our quality team uses profile projectors, tool presetters, gauge blocks, coordinate measuring machines (CMM), and surface roughness testers to verify every tool's precision. Tool trials are documented, and adjustments are made until zero deviation is achieved. Regular maintenance logs and inspection certificates ensure long-term reliability.
